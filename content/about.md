Thinking back, I was almost raised by the internet—the glowing screen felt more real than real. Finding unsecured webcams to mail in warnings, websites with free money at the click of a button, or meeting online strangers to discover a cute and awkward teen. It was a very special time and place.

I've been fortunate to pursue this love first as a hobby and then a career. Otherwise, I can usually be found reading, gaming, or exploring. I love to chat, so do get in touch if you have anything worthwhile to say! Some of my contact details are in the footer while others are available by request.

Above all, I want to keep the internet a place where anyone can do anything.
